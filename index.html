About 01

BIM Coordination · Automation · Technical Communication

I’m James Watts, a BIM and preconstruction professional specializing in glazing and facade systems. My work sits between design, construction, and technology: I develop project-specific 3D models, coordinate changing design information, produce shop and proposal graphics, and create dependable workflows that support estimating, preconstruction, coordination, fabrication, field installation, and operations. With a background in industrial design and English literature, I bring equal attention to how information functions and how it is communicated—translating complicated facade conditions into clear models, diagrams, schedules, and presentations.
